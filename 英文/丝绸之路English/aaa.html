  • The Significance of Constructing "Silk Road Economic Belt"

    Silk Road economic belt is in the "ancient Silk Road" concept based on the formation of a new economic development area. Silk Road economic zone, east of the Asia-Pacific economic circle, west of the European economic circle, is considered "the world's longest, most development potential of the economic corridor." Silk Road economic belt is first a "economic belt" concept, embodies the economic belt of the city focused on the coordinated development of ideas.

    　　

    Most of the countries along the Silk Road are in the "collapse zone" between the two engines. The whole area has the phenomenon of "high and low on both sides". The development of economy and the pursuit of a better life are the prevailing demands of the countries and the people in the region. This demand is in line with the needs of the two major economic engines, together to build the international strategic foundation of the Silk Road economic zone.

    　

    1. China's current development needs to take into account the regional balance, and focus on opening up new economic growth point. Revival of the Silk Road can drive the economic strength of the western region is relatively weak, is expected to form a new frontier.

    　

    2. Through the construction of "Silk Road Economic Zone" for China to create a good surrounding political, national defense, national environment.

    　

    3. Can promote regional interconnection between the various infrastructure, including infrastructure, is conducive to promoting regional cooperation level.

    　

    4. Is conducive to digest the serious excess of China's various production capacity.

    　
    　

    "Silk Road Economic Zone" can be gradually started through the following steps: First, strengthen policy communication. Second, strengthen the road Unicom. Third, to strengthen trade flow. Fourth, strengthen the currency circulation. Fifth, strengthen the people together, to strengthen people's friendly exchanges and social exchanges. Which is the basis of road connectivity, trade is essential to the content.

    　　

    And the five pillars corresponding to the five specific measures include: the development of transport and logistics channel; to achieve trade and investment facilitation, to break the bottleneck of regional economic development; to promote cooperation in the financial sector; the establishment of energy clubs; the establishment of grain cooperation mechanism.

    "Silk Road economic belt" is a transnational economic zone, the long-term goal is to build a new model of regional cooperation. The difference between the "Silk Road Economic Belt" and the traditional regional cooperation model is that traditional regional cooperation is through the establishment of reciprocal trade and investment arrangements, the establishment of a unified tariff policy, and then the establishment of supranational institutions to achieve in-depth cooperation. "Silk Road Economic Zone" did not set high-end goals, the recent major trade, transportation, investment cooperation in the future will not set up customs union. "Economic belt" is not a "close integration organization", will not break the existing regional institutional arrangements, more is a pragmatic and flexible economic cooperation arrangements.

    Silk Road economic belt with a total population of nearly 3 billion, the market size and unique potential. China will be in the "new Silk Road" on the cultivation of new economic growth pole, will be the introduction of industry, gathering population, which will make the western region faster development, and for the central and western provinces of China electromechanical products, specialty agricultural products, specialty food And other goods to the west exports to create a rare opportunity.

    　　

    President Xi Jinping proposed to build the "Silk Road Economic Zone" focus is not just Central Asia, but a larger chess game. What is the greatest value of the idea of "Silk Road economic belt"? Not the trade with the five Central Asian countries, not from the supply of oil and gas resources in Central Asia, but to cope with the turmoil in the Arab world under the pressure of trade routes.
