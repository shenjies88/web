 "The Belt and Road" to promote Sino-Russian power cooperation

In recent years, China and Russia have made many new developments in energy cooperation, driven by the "One Way" initiative. In Russia, the power projects built by Chinese companies benefit the local population and are widely acclaimed.

Jining Kaya Power Station is currently China's largest power and energy investment project in Russia. The power station uses state-of-the-art gas-fired equipment to reduce natural gas consumption by 25% and reduce air pollutant emissions by 30%.Natural gas through the gas unit after combustion into the steam turbine unit to re-cycle, in order to achieve the effective use of energy. As the first power station to be built by the Chinese side in Yaroslavl, Jining Skaya Power Station also provided a platform for Russian staff to learn Chinese technology.

The project will further stimulate the local development potential, which is the model of cooperation and mutual benefit between the two countries. Through cooperation to China's advanced technology, standards and equipment to go out to drive the local economic development, such cooperation is a typical mutual benefit and win-win situation.
